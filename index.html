AI PING PONG GAME









  1. ; First keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. Move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
  3. Move your right fist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  4. Now press the play button,and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
  5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per movement of your right wrist.
